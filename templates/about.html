About Us

What we do

At Workish, we believe that finding a job shouldn't feel like surviving an obstacle course. We listen, we guide, and we work with you to find opportunities that sure where you're headed - not just where you've been.

We're not fans of buzzwords, vague job specs, or being ghosted (don't worry, we won't do it to you either). We prefer plain English, proper feedback, and knowing the difference between a “fast-paced environment” and a chaotic mess. Our job is to cut through the noise, connect great people, and ideally, make you feel a little less like throwing your laptop out the window. We're human, we're helpful, and we're just the right amount of workish.

Our Journey

2019 — Sparked by Frustration

After years of watching people get overlooked, undervalued, or pushed into roles that didn’t fit, Workish began as a side project — a better way to help people find work that works for them.

2020 — First Clients, First Wins

We helped our first candidates land roles they were excited about — not just ones that ticked boxes. And we helped hiring managers cut through noise to meet real talent.

2021 — Word Gets Around

Without advertising, our client list grew. So did our reach. Remote-first companies found us, and we embraced a broader way of working — across sectors and cities.

2023 — Team Workish Expands

We brought on brilliant people who believe in doing things differently. More empathy, more clarity, less waffle.

2025 - Still Small, Still Sharp

We're not chasing scale. We're chasing sense. Work that fits. Hires that last. Conversations that matter.
